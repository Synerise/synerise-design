# ProgressBar

Three progress bar variants for visualising completion, distribution, and segmented data: a single-value segmented bar (`ProgressBar`), a multi-value stacked or side-by-side bar (`Multivalue`), and a colour-coded tile bar (`ProgressTiles`).

## Quick overview

- Import `ProgressBar` (default), `Multivalue`, or `ProgressTiles` from `@synerise/ds-progress-bar`
- `ProgressBar` displays a single percentage, optionally split into `steps` segments
- `Multivalue` renders multiple colour-coded bars ‚Äî stacked (overlapping) by default, or side-by-side with `stackedBars={false}`
- `ProgressTiles` renders fixed-width colour tiles with proportional fill
- All variants accept standard `HTMLDivElement` attributes via `WithHTMLAttributes`

## Notes & tips

- **`inline` suppresses `description`** ‚Äî even if you pass a `description` prop, it won't render when `inline` is `true`.
- **Percent is clamped** ‚Äî `Multivalue` clamps each bar's percent to 0‚Äì100 via `normalizePercent`.
- **Segment fill algorithm** ‚Äî both `ProgressBar` and `ProgressTiles` share the same fill logic: `tileWidthRatio = 100 / steps`, then each tile is filled proportionally.
